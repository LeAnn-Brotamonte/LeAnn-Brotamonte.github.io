About

This is the user interface for Team 48's Persistence of Vision Display system. Directions For Use:

The purpose of this application is to allow users to draw their own designs to be displayed onto the LEDs. To get started, follow the below instructions to begin designing:

  • Press the start button to begin the session
  • With your mouse, click and drag to draw your design.
  • Clear and erase buttons are available if you wish to restart your design or erase a mistake.
  • Press the stop button when you're done and the design is complete.
